Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 9, 2025

Overview

Stamp’d is a mobile app designed to help travelers plan trips, manage packing lists, and share itineraries via email. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data Collection

Stamp’d does not require users to create accounts or submit personal information to use core features. However, when users choose to send trip summaries or packing lists via email, the app temporarily processes:

  • Email addresses entered by the user

No data is stored permanently by Stamp’d. All email content is generated on-device and dispatched via Amazon SES. We do not collect analytics, location data, or device identifiers.

Use of Email

All emails sent by Stamp’d are user-initiated and transactional. The app sends content only to the addresses specified by the user. Stamp’d does not send marketing emails, newsletters, or automated messages.

Third-Party Services

Stamp’d uses Amazon Web Services (SES and SNS) to send and manage email delivery. These services may process metadata for bounce and complaint handling. We do not share user data with any other third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Stamp’d is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been inadvertently collected, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us at jonathan.e.eng@gmail.com to request removal.

Data Security

We use secure protocols and verified domains (SPF, DKIM) to ensure email authenticity and prevent spoofing. No user data is stored on our servers.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data you believe has been collected or processed by contacting us directly. We will respond within 30 days.
