About Stamp’d

Stamp’d is a mobile travel app that helps users plan trips, organize packing lists, and share beautifully formatted trip summaries through email.

How Email Is Used

All emails sent by Stamp’d are user-initiated and transactional. When a traveler exports their itinerary or packing list, the app generates an HTML email (using a predefined template) and sends it only to the addresses they specify.

Stamp’d does not send marketing emails, newsletters, or subscription-based messages. We have configured Amazon SES with Amazon SNS integration to manage bounces and complaints, ensuring email reliability and trust.

Technical Overview

  • Verified domain for authenticated sending (SPF, DKIM configured)
  • Transactional emails only, one per user request
  • Hosted on AWS Lambda & SES for email dispatch
